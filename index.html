Currently, I'm in the process of founding a youth organization called The World in Us (TWIU). TWIU seeks to eliminate cultural ignorance from its roots through youth-for-youth education: we provide a platform for high school and college students to research, prepare, and teach introductory curriculum on global cultures to elementary-aged students.
